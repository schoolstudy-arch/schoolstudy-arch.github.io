ABOUT THE GSMST STUDENT COUNCIL

Who are we?

We are an award winning and nationally recognized Student Council of Excellence whose mission is to serve both the student body and the community. We are made up of 26 total members from Exectutives, Seniors, Juniors, Sophomores, and Freshmen!

What do we do?

We plan and coordinate events through the year and help sponsor other events for groups such as NASH. We also try to improve the livelyhood of the student body by creating recreational events for them to destress and socialize with others!

Key features of our council

Community

We would love to have your feedback on any events and we will take them and try to improve them in the future! We also love working with the student body and invite them to come help during our workdays!

Support

We do our best to support the student body with any hardships through our many events that encourage student participation in school events and through morale boosting!

Passion

We always try our best for the school and the student body. We put 100% into every event that we create for the student body! We have a main monthly meeting to discuss an overview of everything and we always try to keep attendance consistant!

Teamwork

If student council is a machine then the members are the gears that support eachother and work together to make anything work!
