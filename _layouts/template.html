ABOUT SCHOOL STUDY

Who are we?

We are students whose mission are to serve both the student body and the community, so students can perform better. We are made up of 2 total members!

What do we do?

We upload notes, study guides, and other resources to help students!

Key features of School Study

Community

We would love to have your feedback on any events and we will take them and try to improve them in the future! We also love working with the student body and would love for you to help us!

Support

We do our best to support the student body with any hardships through our many resources that encourage student participation in school, academically!

Passion

We always try our best for the school and the student body. We put 100% into every function that we create for the student body! We have a main monthly meeting to discuss an overview of everything and we always try to keep notes consistent!

Teamwork

If school study is a machine then the members are the gears that support eachother and work together to make anything work!
